Let us now be quiet, quiet our souls as we prepare ourselves to be in the presence of Our Lord and our Blessed Mother.

Let us invite them, our group`s guardian angel, and all the angels and saints to pray for and with us. While the hymn to the Holy Spirit is playing, let us meditate on the awesomeness of God and His working in our lives with grateful hearts.

◦  Prayer for the End of the Corona Virus Pandemic

 

May we who are merely inconvenienced, remember those whose lives are at stake.

May we who have no risk factors, remember those most vulnerable.

May we who have the luxury of working from home, remember those who must choose between preserving their health or making their rent.

May we who have the flexibility to care for our children when their schools close, remember those who have no options.

May we who have to cancel our trips, remember those that have no place to go.

May we who are losing our margin money in the tumult of the economic market, remember those who have no margin at all.

May we who settle in for a quarantine at home, remember those who have no home.

During this time when we cannot physically wrap our arms around each other, let us find ways to be the loving embrace of God to our neighbors. Amen.

◦  The Five Luminous Mysteries

The First Luminous Mystery : Jesus’ Baptism in the Jordan

 

Leader: Meditate on: Jesus appeared in the Jordan and insisted John the Baptist baptizes Him. As this occurs, the Holy Spirit descended upon Him in the form of a dove while the Father proclaimed, ``This was my beloved Son with whom I am well pleased.``

 

All: Resolution: As Christ was baptized I will submit also to His Father’s will

 

Leader: With the image of the Baptism of Jesus in mind, we pray

 

Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, Glory be, O my Jesus...

 

All: Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the grace to live the vows of my Baptism in union with Jesus my Lord.

 

Sing: Ave, Ave, Ave Maria...

 

◦    The Second Luminous Mystery : Jesus’ Miracle at the Wedding in Cana

Leader: Meditate on: When Christ changed water into wine, we see You, Mary, in guise of a teacher and intercessor as you urge the servants to do what Jesus commands.

.

 

All: Resolution: As Mary tells people to ``Do whatever He tells you,`` I will do what Christ wants of me.

Leader: With the image of the Wedding Feast in Cana in mind, we pray

Our Father... 10 Hail Mary... Glory be... O My Jesus...

 

All: Our Lady of the Rosary, please intercede for me to overcome the temptations of the world, the flesh and the devil so that I may experience the joy of the Lord’s presence, power and love.

 

Sing: Ave, Ave, Ave Maria...

 

◦    The Third Luminous Mystery : Jesus’ Proclamation of the Kingdom of God

Leader: Meditate on: As Jesus announced the Kingdom, He called all to conversion and forgiveness of sins, saying, ``I came not to call the righteous, but sinners.``

 

All: Resolution: I will strive to be worthy to enter Christ’s Kingdom through His example.

 

Leader: With the image of Jesus’ Proclamation of the Kingdom of God in mind,

we pray...

Our Father... 10 Hail Mary... Glory be... O my Jesus...

 

All: Our Lady of the Rosary , please obtain for me from your Son true and perfect contrition for my sins, sincere sacramental confession in humble trust, and the Grace to amend my life for love of God.

 

Sing: Ave, Ave, Ave Maria...

 

◦    The Fourth Luminous Mystery : The Transfiguration

Leader: Meditate on: The Transfiguration shows the glory of the Godhead shining forth from the face of Christ. Peter, James and John witnessed Jesus’ face and clothes become dazzling with light, as He spoke with Moses and Elijah.

 

All: Resolution: As God revealed Himself to us, I will live my life through Christ always.

 

Leader: With the image of the Transfiguration in mind, we pray

Our Father... 10 Hail Mary... Glory be... O My Jesus...

 

All: Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from Your Son the grace of His light here on earth so as to enjoy His glory in Heaven.

 

Sing: Ave, Ave, Ave Maria...

◦    The Fifth Luminous Mystery : Jesus’ Institution of the Holy Eucharist

Leader: Meditate on: Christ offers His Body and Blood as food under the signs of bread and wine, instituting a sacrificial memorial of His death and resurrection for the salvation of the whole world.

 

All: Resolution: I will receive Christ regularly at Holy Mass and consecrate myself to him forever.

 

Leader: With the image of Jesus instituting the Holy Eucharist in mind, we pray

Our Father... 10 Hail Mary... Glory be... O my Jesus...

 

All: Our Lady of the Rosary, please obtain for me from your Son the Grace to receive Him often in the sacramental and spiritual Holy Communion, always worthily and in the spirit of thanksgiving.

 

Sing: Ave, Ave, Ave Maria...

◦  Hail Holy Queen

All: Hail, Holy Queen, Mother of Mercy, our life, our sweetness and our hope. To thee do we cry poor banished children of Eve. To thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this valley of tears. Turn then most gracious advocate thine eyes of mercy towards us, and after this our exile, show unto us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus. O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary.

 

Leader: Pray for us, O holy mother of God.

All: That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.

 

Let us pray: O God, whose only begotten Son, by His life, death and resurrection, has purchased for us the rewards of eternal life, grant, we beseech you, that meditating on these mysteries of the Most Holy Rosary of the Blessed Virgin Mary, we may imitate what they contain and obtain what they promise, through the same Christ our Lord. Amen.

◦   Consecration to the Sacred Heart of Jesus

Sacred Heart of Jesus, with infinite love,

broken by my ingratitude,

Pierced by my sins, yet loving me still,

Accept the consecration that I make to You,

Of all that I am and all that I have.

Take every faculty of my soul and body,

And draw me day by day,

Nearer and nearer to Your Sacred Side,

And there, as I can bear the lesson,

Teach me Your blessed ways.

Amen.

◦   Consecration to the Virgin Mary

 

My Queen and my Mother, I give myself entirely to you;

And to show my devotion to you,

I consecrate to you this day my eyes, my ears, my mouth, my heart,

My whole being without reserve.

Wherefore, good Mother, as I am your own,

Keep me, guard me, as your property and possession. Amen.

 

◦  Farewell Prayer

O beloved Mother, the time has come for your departure from our home,

your home also for 14 days of your stay with us.

Our lips cannot express the joy overflowing in our hearts.

Inspired by your Love, Mother, you desired to be with us even for only a while. Thank you, Mother, thank you. Come back some other day.

We will wait for you with profound ardor.

Upon your departure, take with you our love, take with your our hearts. Never allow us to forget you nor to stray away from your care now and forever. Amen.

◦  Welcome Prayer

Our Mother and mother of Christ, we are happy and honored by your visit in our home which you made worthy to be the bode of your love and affection. During these days that you will stay with us, please bless our families and always abide with us through your graces.

Give us, beloved mother your blessing, bless this home and those members of our families who are away, and deliver us from all evil.

From now on, we are consecrating our minds, our hearts and our works to you, since you are our mother, and grant that we may comply with the commandments of your Son, Jesus Christ, and with the commandments of Mother Church. We pledge to make this visit arouse in us a true love of God and to lead us to a true devotion to you, so that we may fulfill our duties to ourselves, to our neighbors and to society. May peace reign in the world!
